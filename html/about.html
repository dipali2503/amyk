    About Us

    Amyrk International Processors, Inc. is an employee-owned, multi-faceted tobacco product and service organization in the United States. As a tobacco seller, purchaser and processor, we offer tobacco products and services to meet the needs of the tobacco industry at every phase of production and manufacturing.

    The expert team at Amyrk International includes some of the most knowledgeable and experienced professionals in the tobacco industry. Their decades of knowledge and experience combine with the company's commitment to innovation and excellence to put Amyrk International at the forefront of the tobacco industry.

    Amyrk International Processors, Inc. has made it a priority to employ the best minds and experts is knowledgeable and experienced with every phase of tobacco production from barn to burn.

    Our employees enable Amyrk International Processors to offer a wide range of tobacco product and services to help you achieve efficiency, value, and superior results.
